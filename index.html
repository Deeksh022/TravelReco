Popular Destinations

Beach Escapes

Maldives Serenity

Overwater villas, turquoise lagoons, and calm nights under starlit skies make this a perfect beach hideaway.

Best time: November to April

Phuket Getaway

Blend jungle trekking with island hopping around lively beach towns and secluded coves.

Try: snorkeling around Phi Phi Islands

Temple Trail

Angkor Wat, Cambodia

Explore the sunrise over ancient stones, ornate galleries, and peaceful reflecting ponds inside the temple complex.

Tip: Rent a bike for early morning visits

Kyoto, Japan

Experience zen gardens, incense-scented tea houses, and the breathtaking Fushimi Inari gate-lined paths.

Season: Cherry blossoms in spring

Country Highlights

Switzerland — Alpine Serenity

Charming villages, pristine lakes, and efficient railways make this ideal for scenic road trips and mountain hikes.

Must-see: Interlaken & Lucerne

Italy — Culture & Cuisine

From Tuscan vineyards to Amalfi cliffside towns, immerse in art, food, and coastal charm across the boot-shaped peninsula.

Enjoy: Sicilian seafood feasts
